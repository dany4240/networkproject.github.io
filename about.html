Proposed Network Topology

The best network topology for this startup is Star Topology

The star topology is ideal for a smart office due to its centralized structure, which makes it reliable, scalable, and easy to troubleshoot. In this design, all devices connect directly to a central network device (switch/router). This topology is cost-effective and supports both wired and wireless communication.

Network Communication Media

Wired Connections:

  • Category 6 (Cat6) Ethernet Cables: Used for reliable high-speed connections to PCs, printers, cameras and servers.

Wireless Connections:

  • TP-Link ER7206: Used for laptops and mobile devices, ensuring fast speeds and low latency.

Star Topology Advantage

Easy to manage:

  • Since all devices are connected to a central hub or switch, troubleshooting and managing the network is easier. If a device or cable fails, it doesn't affect other devices.

Scalability:

  • Adding new devices to the network is simple. You just need to connect them to the central hub, without disrupting the entire network.

Centralized Control:

  • The central hub or switch allows for centralized control of the network, which can improve performance monitoring, security, and the ability to implement network-wide policies.

Fault Isolation:

  • If one device or cable fails, it won't disrupt the entire network, unlike bus or ring topologies where a failure can affect the whole network.
